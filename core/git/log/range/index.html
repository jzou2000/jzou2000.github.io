Range

By Revision

rev1..rev2

revision from rev1 (excluded) to rev2 (included)

rev~ and rev^

Suffix ~ to a revision parameter means the parent of that commit object. rev~<n> means the n-th generation ancestor of rev.

Suffix ^ also means the parent of the commit object, so rev^ is the same with rev~. However, rev^<n> means the n-th parent if rev has multiple parents, such as a merging commit.

rev^..rev

Show only rev, i.e. between before rev to rev, or equivalent to rev -1

By Time

--since

--since=2.weeks commits in the last 2 weeks

--until

--until=2008-01-05 commits until the date 2008-01-05
