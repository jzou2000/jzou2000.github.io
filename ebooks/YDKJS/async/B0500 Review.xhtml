Review

Promises and generators provide the foundational building blocks upon which we can build much more sophisticated and capable asynchrony.

asynquence has utilities for implementing iterable sequences, reactive sequences (“Observables”), concurrent coroutines, and even CSP goroutines.

Those patterns, combined with the continuation-callback and Promise capabilities, give asynquence a powerful mix of different asynchronous functionalities, all integrated in one clean async flow control abstraction: the sequence.
